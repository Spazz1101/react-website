My CV

I am an applied microeconomist with interests in behavioral, health, environmental, and public economics.
My research uses behavioral and experimental methods to examine questions related to public health and environmental issues. I seek to conduct research on established and emerging issues related to agriculture, public health, and environmental issues to inform both private and public decision making.
